Passport Mela


Passport is an indispensable and important document for students seeking to go abroad for higher education. Passport Mela is a tailor made process to help students get their passports done in a hassle free and convenient way in just three simple steps:
  1. Online Registration
  2. Document Verification at Passport Seva Kendra Mangaluru
  3. Police Verification

WHY PASSPORT MELA?

  • Hassle free 3-step process
  • Regular updates via SMS
  • Free Transport service
  • 24 X 7 Helpline
  • Student organised with no third party involved
  • Tailor made process to suit your academic schedule
